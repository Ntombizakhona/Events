GitHub Constellation
====================

Johannesburg
------------

GitHub Constellation 2024 was an in-person developer conference held on 14 November 2024, at The Galleria in Sandton, Johannesburg, South Africa. This event celebrated the South African developer community, focusing on themes like AI, security, collaboration, and community engagement.

Keynote and Featured Speakers
-----------------------------

The conference featured a keynote address by GitHub’s CEO, Thomas Dohmke. Other notable speakers included:

### Lebogang Madise

Account Technology Strategist at Microsoft.

### Tiyani Nghonyama

COO at Geekulcha.

### Demetris Cheatham

Chief of Staff to the CEO at GitHub.

### Riaz Moola

Founder and CEO at HyperionDev.

Event Highlights
----------------

The conference agenda encompassed various sessions and activities:

### AI Discussions

Exploring the impact of generative AI, strategies for responsible adoption, and future trends.

### Security Workshops

Focusing on proactive security measures, AI-driven security features, and compliance standards.

### Developer Experience Sessions

Addressing collaboration, tech stack simplification, and developer-first security practices.

### Community Engagement

Highlighting open source contributions, educational initiatives, and social impact projects.

Hackathon and Training
----------------------

A notable feature of the event was a hackathon aimed at developing AI-driven solutions to societal challenges in South Africa. Participants were encouraged to collaborate and innovate, with the top teams presenting their projects on the main stage.

Additionally, a GitHub Copilot training session was conducted to help developers maximize the tool’s potential.

Community Engagement
--------------------

The event fostered community involvement through various channels:

### Exclusive Preview Livestream

Prior to the conference, a virtual meetup provided insights into the event’s objectives and featured appearances by some speakers.

### Social Media Interaction

Attendees and organizers shared updates and experiences on platforms like Instagram, enhancing community engagement.

GitHub Constellation 2024 in Johannesburg not only showcased the vibrancy of the South African developer community but also emphasized GitHub’s commitment to supporting and celebrating developers across the continent.
