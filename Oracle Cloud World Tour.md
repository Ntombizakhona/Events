Oracle CloudWorld Tour
======================

Dubai
-----


Oracle’s CloudWorld Tour 2025 commenced in Dubai on 22 January 2025, marking the beginning of a global series aimed at showcasing the latest advancements in Cloud Technology and AI Integration.

Highlights from Dubai
---------------------

### Keynote Presentations

Oracle executives unveiled new AI-driven cloud services designed to enhance business operations and efficiency.

### Customer Success Stories

Regional businesses shared their experiences of leveraging Oracle’s cloud solutions to drive innovation and growth.

### Hands-On Workshops

Participants engaged in interactive sessions, exploring Oracle’s AI capabilities and cloud infrastructure.

Upcoming Events
---------------

The CloudWorld Tour continues across various cities, offering opportunities for professionals to engage with Oracle’s latest technologies:

### São Paulo

25 March 2025, at the World Trade Center São Paulo

### Bogotá

1 April 2025, at the Ágora Bogotá Convention Center

### Ciudad de México

9 April 2025, at Expo Santa Fe México

### Miami

1 May 2025, at the Mandarin Oriental

### New York City

6 May 2025, at the Hilton Midtown

Each event is tailored to provide insights into Oracle’s cloud innovations, with sessions focusing on AI integration, infrastructure advancements, and customer success narratives.

For those interested in attending, registration details and event specifics are available on Oracle’s official CloudWorld Tour page.

The tour will culminate with the flagship Oracle CloudWorld conference in **Las Vegas from 13 October to 16 October 2025**, bringing together global industry leaders and technology enthusiasts.
