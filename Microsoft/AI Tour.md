Microsoft AI Tour
=================

Johannesburg
------------


On 23 January 2025, Johannesburg hosted the Microsoft AI Tour, a significant event uniting business leaders, developers, and AI professionals to delve into the latest advancements in artificial intelligence.

Event Highlights
----------------

### Keynote Address

Lillian Barnard, President of Microsoft Africa, announced an ambitious initiative to equip one million South Africans with AI and cybersecurity skills by 2026. This program aims to empower individuals across various sectors, including businesses, government, and youth, providing them with valuable training and industry-recognized certifications.

### Hands-On Workshops:

Participants engaged in interactive sessions, exploring practical applications of AI technologies and gaining insights into integrating AI solutions within their organizations.

### Networking Opportunities

The event facilitated connections among attendees, fostering collaborations and discussions on AI-driven innovations tailored to the African context.
